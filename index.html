The History of Donald J. Trump

Tracking the daily political history of one of the most unconventional figures in American politics.

Welcome to thehistoryofdonaldjtrump.com. This project documents Donald J. Trump's daily political activities, decisions, public statements, and major events throughout his time in public life. Donald Trump is a unique political figure - one who has changed the way politics operates in the United States. This archive seeks to provide a chronological record for historians, journalists, researchers, and curious citizens.
